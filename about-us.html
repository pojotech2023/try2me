About Us

About Try2Me

At VCS Prema Enterprises, we believe that a well-maintained washing machine leads to cleaner clothes and a healthier home. That’s why we created Try2me, a powerful yet easy-to-use descaling powder designed to keep your washing machine in top condition.

Limescale, detergent residue, and grime build up over time, reducing your machine’s efficiency and leaving behind unpleasant odors. Try2me effectively dissolves these stubborn deposits, ensuring every wash is as fresh and effective as the first.

Our product is safe for all types of washing machines and has been formulated with efficiency and convenience in mind. Whether you use a top-load, front-load, or semi-automatic machine, Try2me keeps it running smoothly, extending its lifespan and enhancing its performance.

We are committed to providing high-quality cleaning solutions that make household maintenance easier. With Try2me, you get a hassle-free solution to keep your machine fresh, hygienic, and long-lasting.

Our Misson

At Try2me, our mission is to provide innovative descaling solutions that keep washing machines running efficiently, hygienically, and for longer. We aim to offer easy-to-use, eco-friendly products that ensure cleaner clothes and higher customer satisfaction.
